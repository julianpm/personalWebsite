About Me

My name is Julian. I love to listen to vinyl records. I started buying records in 2004 when I was a student at the University of Western Ontario in London. The only turntable I have ever used is a vintage 1979 Sony that I liberated from my parents' house. I currently have between 350 and 400 records. I mainly collect heavy metal, hip hop, punk rock, and jazz. Below I will show you some of my favourite records from each of the above genres, with a few miscellaneous gems thrown in for good measure.

"When I was younger, I'd buy a vinyl album, take it home and live with it, and I think that attachment's largely gone for the file-sharing generation."

—Anton Corbijn

"The scary thing is when I did my set in Texas everyone was excited. The show was great. I was done and the next DJ put something on vinyl and the difference! The quality!!"

—Peter Hook
